ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The Apostle Paul and his companions crossed the Roman Empire to spread the Gospel and strengthen the churches they founded. This map overlays their journeys onto an interactive view of Roman provinces, settlements, and the all-important road network. It shows the likely paths over land and sea, drawing from the Digital Atlas of the Roman Empire and other research listed below. Click here for more details.

This is an independent project by Robert Rouse, creator of Viz.Bible. It’s part of a vision to connect the people, places, periods, and passages of the Bible in ways that current applications fall short. Please consider becoming a patron to support these efforts.
